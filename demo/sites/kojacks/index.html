Under New Ownership

A Fresh Chapter for Kojack's

Kojack's Chinese Restaurant has been a cornerstone of Shawville and the greater Pontiac region for years. Now, under exciting new ownership, we are bringing renewed energy, fresh recipes, and a deep commitment to quality and community to every table. Same beloved address, brand-new experience.

What We Offer

Dining Your Way

Whether you prefer a relaxed sit-down meal or the convenience of takeout, Kojack's is here to serve the Shawville and Pontiac community with authentic Chinese flavours.

Dine-In

Enjoy a full sit-down dining experience in our welcoming restaurant. Perfect for family meals, dates, or a relaxed evening out.

Takeout

Call ahead and pick up your favourite dishes. Quick, convenient, and just as delicious — your comfort food, your way.

Lunch Specials

Midday value-packed combo plates that hit the spot without breaking the bank. Ask us about today's lunch specials.

Group & Family Dining

Planning a gathering? Our menu is perfect for sharing. Family-style portions and group-friendly seating available.

Combination Plates

Can't decide? Our combo plates let you enjoy multiple dishes in one generous serving — always a crowd favourite.
