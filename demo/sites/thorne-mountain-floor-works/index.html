What We Do

Our Services

01
Installation

Hardwood Floor Installation

Solid and engineered hardwood installation is our cornerstone service. Every plank is measured, cut, fitted, and fastened with the precision that only 30 years of experience can provide. We work with all species — oak, maple, cherry, walnut, pine — and all installation methods.

  • Solid & engineered hardwood
  • All species and plank widths
  • Nail-down, glue-down, floating
  • Residential & commercial
Most Popular Service
02
Installation

Vinyl Plank Flooring

Luxury vinyl plank offers the warmth and beauty of hardwood with superior moisture resistance and durability — perfect for every room in your home. We install all major brands and profiles, with precise fitting and clean transitions.

  • Waterproof core LVP and LVT
  • Kitchens, bathrooms, basements
  • Wide range of wood & stone looks
  • Budget-friendly and long-lasting
Great Value
03
Restoration

Floor Sanding

Years of foot traffic, pet scratches, and wear take their toll on hardwood floors. Our professional sanding service strips away the damage and levels the surface to reveal the beautiful wood beneath — ready for a fresh finish.

  • Drum and orbital sanding
  • Dust-minimized process
  • Edge and corner detail work
  • Ready-for-finish surface guaranteed
Floor Restoration
04
Finishing

Staining & Finishing

The finish is what transforms a sanded floor into a showpiece. We offer a full range of custom stain colours and premium topcoats — water-based or oil-based, matte, satin, or high-gloss. We help you select the tone that brings your vision to life.

  • Full custom stain colour consultation
  • Oil-based and water-based finishes
  • Matte, satin, and gloss topcoats
  • Built for durability and daily life
Custom Colours Available
