What We Do

Our Services

From raw subfloor to flawless finish, we handle every aspect of hardwood and vinyl flooring. Each project is treated with the same care we\'d bring to our own home.

01
Hardwood Floor Installation
Solid and engineered hardwood installation with precision fitting, expert nailing or gluing, and a finish that enhances the natural character of the wood.
Residential & Commercial
02
Vinyl Flooring Installation
Luxury vinyl plank and tile installation — durable, moisture-resistant, and available in a wide range of wood and stone looks. Perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and high-traffic areas.
All Room Types
03
Floor Sanding
Restore worn, scratched, or uneven hardwood floors to a smooth, level surface ready for finishing. We use professional-grade equipment for a dust-minimized process.
Floor Restoration
04
Staining & Finishing
Custom stain colours and premium finish coats — oil-based, water-based, matte, satin, or gloss. We help you find the tone that fits your space and stands up to daily life.
Custom Colours
05
Floor Repairs
Squeaky boards, gaps, cracked planks, or damaged sections — we diagnose the root cause and fix it properly so the problem doesn\'t return.
Diagnosis Included
06
Subfloor Preparation
A great floor starts with a solid foundation. We level, clean, and prepare subfloors to ensure your new surface performs and looks perfect for the long haul.
Foundation First
