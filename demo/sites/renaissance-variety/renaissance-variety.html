Our Story

More Than a Shop.
A Collection.

Renaissance Variety is Shawville’s go-to entertainment destination — a treasure-packed universe of video games, books, movies, and music spanning every decade and every genre.

Whether you’re hunting for a rare NES cartridge, a classic novel, a vintage LP, or the latest release — we have it at prices that make it impossible to leave empty-handed.

Inspired by the spirit of adventure — just like the murals on our walls — every visit is a new discovery.
