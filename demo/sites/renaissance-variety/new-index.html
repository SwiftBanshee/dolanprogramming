Our Story

More Than a Shop.
A Collection.

Renaissance Variety is Shawville’s go-to entertainment destination — a treasure-packed universe of video games, books, movies, and music spanning every decade and every genre.

Whether you’re hunting for a rare NES cartridge, a first-edition novel, a vintage LP, or the latest release — we have it at prices that make it impossible to leave empty-handed.

Every visit is a new discovery. Come in and see what’s landed on the floor this week.
