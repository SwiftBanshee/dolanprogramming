Our Story

A Labour of Love Since 2002

Since our official opening in October 2002, we've made it our mission to provide wholesome, nourishing foods prepared with the finest ingredients — exactly as you would in your own home.

At Café 349 we serve fresh, and often home-grown, ingredients in stylish presentations of generous proportions. We offer a full menu alongside ever-changing "featured lunches" designed to delight your taste buds every visit.

The combination of delicious homemade food and hand-crafted artwork creates a dining experience in perfect harmony with the café's tranquil atmosphere and inviting décor. We are continuously striving to improve and expand what we offer our community.

Private Events

Farm-to-Table for Your Special Day

Since October 2002 we have made it our mission to provide wholesome foods prepared with the finest ingredients, as you would in your own home. We proudly support local farms, often using fresh produce from farm gate to your dinner plate.

With elegant surroundings, we can comfortably seat up to 35 people for a delicious served or buffet meal. We can also provide catering delivered to your home or community hall — we'd be delighted to discuss your event and craft the perfect menu together.

Fully personalized menus can be emailed or picked up at the café. A non-refundable 10% deposit is required at booking. Payment accepted by debit, Visa, MasterCard, cash, cheque, or e-transfer.
