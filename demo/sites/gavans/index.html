Our Story

A Tradition Runs Deep

For over seven decades, Gavan's Hotel has been the heart of Quyon — a place where friends, families, and travelers come together. Originally dubbed the “Gateway to the Pontiac” by founder Lennox Gavan, this beloved establishment has been a proud ambassador of the region’s rich music, food, and culture since 1946.

Still family-owned and deeply rooted in tradition, Gavan’s continues to welcome generations of guests who gather for ice-cold drinks, hearty meals, unforgettable live shows, and cherished memories. Make Gavan’s your tradition — because here, you are family.
