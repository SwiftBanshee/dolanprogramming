Our Story

More Than a Meal — It's Community

Nestled on Main Street in Shawville, Quebec, Hursty's Bar & Grill has long been the gathering place that neighbours count on. Whether you're stopping in after a morning drive through the Pontiac, meeting friends for a midday bite, or settling in for a hearty supper, Hursty's delivers honest, satisfying food every single time.

From our signature burgers and golden wings to the soup of the day and Mom's homemade pie, every item on the menu is made with care. Our friendly team is part of what makes Hursty's special — you'll always be greeted with a smile and treated like family.

We're proud to be a fully licensed establishment with a comfortable summer patio, making us the perfect spot for groups, families, and anyone who simply wants a great meal in great company right in the heart of Shawville, QC.

What We Offer

Our Services

From your first coffee of the morning to a late Sunday supper, Hursty's has something for everyone, every day.

Breakfast

Start your day right with a classic morning meal. We open early Monday through Saturday at 7:00 AM (8:00 AM Sundays) so you never miss the most important meal of the day.

Lunch

Whether you're on a break or catching up with friends, our midday menu offers soups, sandwiches, subs, burgers, and more — fresh and ready for you.

Supper

End your day on a high note with our evening dinner selection — from chicken dinners and pork chops to our signature 10 oz New York Sirloin. Open Sundays until 9:00 PM.

Dine-In

Settle in and enjoy a meal in our welcoming, comfortable dining room. Licensed dining is available, and our summer patio is a neighbourhood favourite when the weather's right.

Takeout

In a hurry? Our full menu is available to take out. Call ahead at (819) 647-2546 and we'll have your order hot and ready for when you arrive.

Group & Family Dining

Hursty's is a family-friendly destination perfect for groups of all sizes. Whether it's a team lunch, family gathering, or community get-together, we're happy to accommodate you.
