Our Services

From small residential lifts to large-scale industrial projects, Tracy Crane Service has the expertise and equipment to handle any job safely and on time across the Pontiac and Outaouais region.

Mobile crane in action
01

Mobile Crane Rental

Our modern mobile crane fleet is available for rental with or without a certified operator. We match the right equipment to your project requirements, whether it's a small residential placement or a major industrial lift.

  • Certified operators available
  • Various tonnage capacities
  • All-terrain and rough terrain options
  • Daily, weekly, and project rates
Rigging / hoisting photo
02

Rigging & Hoisting

Precision rigging for any load type. Our rigging specialists plan each lift carefully, accounting for load weight, centre of gravity, site conditions, and safety clearances to execute a smooth, controlled hoist every time.

  • Heavy and oversized load specialists
  • Custom rigging design
  • Spreader bars and lifting frames
  • Safety-first methodology
Heavy transport photo
03

Heavy Equipment Transport

We move heavy and oversized equipment across the region efficiently and safely. Our transport services include permit handling, route planning, and experienced flagging — so your equipment arrives on time and undamaged.

  • Excavators, bulldozers, machinery
  • Oversized load permits
  • Route surveys available
  • Serving Pontiac and Outaouais
Construction site photo
04

Construction Lifting

Crane support for construction projects of all scales. Steel erection, concrete placement, mechanical equipment installation, and precast lifts — our operators integrate with your crew and keep the schedule moving.

  • Steel and structural erection
  • Precast and tilt-up panel lifts
  • Mechanical equipment placement
  • ICI and residential construction
Residential lift photo
05

Residential Lifts

Rooftop HVAC units, modular home placement, garage packages, timber frames, hot tubs — if it needs to be lifted at your home, we handle it carefully. We respect your property and work efficiently to minimize disruption.

  • HVAC and rooftop placements
  • Modular and prefab home setting
  • Timber frame lifts
  • Minimal property disruption
Industrial / commercial site photo
06

Industrial & Commercial

Specialized lifting for plants, mills, warehouses, and large commercial developments. We understand the demands of industrial environments — tight deadlines, complex loads, and zero tolerance for downtime.

  • Plant maintenance and shutdown lifts
  • Large commercial steel erection
  • Tank and vessel placements
  • Infrastructure projects

Our Story

Built on Trust

Tracy Crane Service has been the go-to lifting and rigging company in Shawville, Quebec for over two decades. We are a local, family-oriented operation that takes pride in showing up prepared, working safely, and getting the job done right — every single time.

From our first residential lifts to complex industrial projects, our commitment to the community and to safety has never wavered. We know the Pontiac region because we live and work here — and that makes all the difference.

Fully licensed and insured operators serving all of Pontiac County and surrounding areas.

Well-maintained fleet with current safety certifications and regular inspection records.

Bilingual team ready to serve both English and French-speaking clients across Quebec.

On-time delivery, transparent pricing, and safety-first culture on every job site.
