About the Artist

A passion for colour and canvas

Cheryl Dolan is a Quebec-based fine art painter whose work captures the beauty of everyday life through vibrant colour, expressive brushwork, and a deep emotional connection to her subjects.

From landscapes that breathe with light to intimate portraits and still lifes, each piece is created with care, intention, and a love of the craft. Commissions are warmly welcomed.
