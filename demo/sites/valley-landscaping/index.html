What We Do

Complete Landscaping Solutions

From the first concept sketch to the final flower planted — Valley Landscaping handles every aspect of your outdoor transformation.

Lawn Care & Maintenance

Regular mowing, edging, fertilization, aeration, overseeding, and full seasonal lawn care programs tailored to Simcoe County's climate.

Hardscaping & Patios

Custom interlocking stone patios, pathways, driveways, and outdoor living spaces built with premium materials for lasting beauty.

Garden Design & Planting

Perennial and annual garden beds, native plant installations, foundation plantings, and colourful seasonal arrangements.

Retaining Walls

Functional and decorative retaining walls using natural stone, interlocking block, and timber — engineered to last.

Water Features

Serene ponds, fountains, waterfalls, and irrigation systems that bring life and sound to your outdoor sanctuary.

Tree & Shrub Care

Professional planting, pruning, trimming, and removal services. Keeping your trees and shrubs healthy and beautiful year-round.

Snow Removal

Reliable commercial and residential snow plowing, salting, and winter maintenance contracts throughout Simcoe County.

Landscape Design

Complete design packages with 3D renderings, site planning, and phased implementation plans to fit your timeline and budget.

Outdoor Lighting

LED landscape lighting for pathways, feature trees, patios, and security — enhancing beauty and safety after dark.

Our Story

Rooted in Community

Valley Landscaping was founded right here in Simcoe County with one simple goal: to create beautiful, lasting outdoor spaces that our clients love. Over two decades later, that commitment hasn’t wavered.

We’re proud to be a local business serving Orillia, Barrie, Collingwood, Midland, Innisfil, Muskoka, and the communities in between. Our team brings passion, expertise, and genuine care to every project.

When you choose Valley Landscaping, you’re partnering with your neighbours who take pride in making this region more beautiful, one yard at a time.
