Our Story

A Family Legacy of Craftsmanship

Founded in 1985, Construction Ghislain Morin has grown into one of the most trusted general contracting companies in the Quebec–Ontario corridor. What started as a passion for building has evolved into a full-service construction company capable of handling any scale of project.

We specialize in coordinating every aspect of construction — architects, engineers, specialized contractors, and suppliers — ensuring your project stays on schedule and within budget. Our commitment to transparency, daily communication, and uncompromising quality has earned us hundreds of glowing testimonials.

  • Custom floor plan design & coordination
  • Solar panel & energy-efficient builds

  • Cottage & year-round modern builds

Log Homes

Authentic Handcrafted Log Homes

We are experienced builders in the Confederation Log and Timber Frame network. Our log homes combine old-world craftsmanship with modern construction techniques for homes that last generations.

  • Custom log home design & assembly
  • Confederation log & timber kits
  • Log cottage & 4-season builds
  • Restoration of existing log homes
  • Full interior finishing & detailing
Request a Quote
Timber Frame Homes

Soaring Timbers, Lasting Beauty

Timber frame construction creates breathtaking open spaces with exposed structural beams that become the focal point of your home. We work with top suppliers to deliver frames that are both structurally superior and visually stunning.

  • Exposed heavy timber framing
  • Custom structural design coordination
  • Open concept great rooms
  • Hybrid timber & conventional builds
  • Interior timber feature elements
Request a Quote
Renovation
