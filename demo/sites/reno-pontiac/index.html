Rooted in Pontiac. Built on Trust.

What We Do

Our Services

Full-scope renovation services for homeowners and businesses in Pontiac County and the Outaouais region.

Kitchen Renovations

Custom cabinetry, countertop installation, layout reconfigurations, and complete kitchen overhauls tailored to your lifestyle and budget.

Bathroom Renovations

Tiling, vanity upgrades, walk-in showers, soaker tubs, plumbing modifications, and full bathroom redesigns from start to finish.

Basement Finishing

Transform unfinished basements into livable spaces — home offices, gyms, entertainment rooms, or in-law suites.

Roofing

Asphalt shingle installation and replacement, metal roofing, flat roof systems, repairs, and full re-roofing for residential and commercial properties.

Exterior Siding & Cladding

Vinyl, wood, and fibre cement siding installation. Soffit, fascia, and eavestroughs replaced and repaired to protect your home.

Flooring

Hardwood, laminate, vinyl plank, ceramic tile, and carpet — supply and installation for every room in your home.

Interior Finishing

Drywall, taping, mudding, painting, trim work, and all interior finishing to bring your renovation to a polished, professional standard.

Decks & Outdoor Structures

Custom deck design and construction, pergolas, gazebos, and outdoor living structures built to withstand Quebec winters.

General Contracting

Full project management from permits to final walkthrough. Additions, home staging, post-disaster renovations, and complete home rebuilds.
