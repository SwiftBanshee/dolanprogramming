Our Story

Over 40 Years of Concrete Excellence

Campbell's Bay Ciment was established in June 1982 as a company devoted to supplying ready-mix concrete to the construction market in Pontiac County. We now serve clients across West Quebec, Outaouais, and the Ottawa Valley.

Since entering the poured concrete foundation sector in 1987 and expanding into aggregate production during the 1990s, we have grown into a full-service concrete and aggregate company serving both residential and commercial markets.

A proud member of the McGrimmon Holdings family of companies, with operations spanning both Quebec and Ontario.

What We Do

Our Services

From foundation pours to stone slinging, mobile crushing to concrete pumping — we have the equipment and expertise to handle projects of any size across the Ottawa Valley.

Poured Concrete Foundations
Residential and commercial poured concrete foundations by our RBQ-licensed crew with decades of expertise in the Ottawa Valley.
Dump Truck Services
Reliable dump truck delivery and haulage for aggregates, fill material, and construction supplies throughout Pontiac and the Ottawa Valley.
Mobile Crushing
On-site mobile crushing to process rock and aggregate directly at your project location, saving time and transport costs.
Concrete Pumping
Pump concrete to hard-to-reach areas and elevated heights. Ideal for walls, floors, and foundations where direct truck access is not possible.
Boom Truck Services
Boom truck capabilities for lifting and placing materials on-site, supporting construction crews across Quebec and Ontario.
Stone Slinger
Precision stone slinging for hard-to-access areas. Ideal for drainage, driveways, back fills, grade fills, parking lots, and steep slopes.
Drainage Driveways Back Fills Parking Lots Landscaping
ICF – Styrorail
Insulated Concrete Form construction using Styrorail for energy-efficient, structurally superior walls in residential and commercial builds.
