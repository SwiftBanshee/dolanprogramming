What We Do

Professional Tree Services

From routine trimming to complex removals and emergency response, Shane's Tree Service delivers expert workmanship every time.

Tree Removal

Safe, efficient removal of hazardous, dead, or unwanted trees of any size. We clear the site completely — leaving no mess behind.

Tree Trimming & Pruning

Shape, health, and safety — we prune to promote strong growth, improve aesthetics, and eliminate dangerous overhanging branches.

Stump Grinding

Eliminate unsightly stumps and reclaim your yard. We grind stumps below ground level so you can replant, pave, or landscape with ease.

24hr Emergency Services

Storm damage, fallen trees, or dangerous limbs over power lines — we're available around the clock to protect your home and property.

Lot & Land Clearing

Preparing land for construction, agriculture, or development? We clear trees, brush, and stumps efficiently to get your project started right.

Tree Health Assessment

Concerned about a tree on your property? Shane will evaluate for disease, structural weakness, and risk — with honest advice on what to do next.
