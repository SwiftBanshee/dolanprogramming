Our Story

A Family Legacy Born in Shawville

Brooklyn Place has its roots in Shawville's community fabric going all the way back to 1999, when Bonnie Harris first opened its doors as Video One — a beloved local video rental shop that became a neighbourhood gathering place.

As times changed, Bonnie's passion for community and bringing smiles to people's faces led her to transform the shop into what it is today: a vibrant ice cream parlour and treat destination right here on Rue Main in Shawville, Quebec.

"The name Brooklyn Place is close to my heart — it's named after my granddaughter Brooklyn. Every scoop we serve is a little tribute to the joy she brings to our family." — Bonnie Harris

Today, Brooklyn Place is more than just an ice cream shop. It's a cherished piece of Shawville life, where locals come to celebrate milestones, beat the heat, pick up Fusion Mineral Paint for a home project, and enjoy the warmth of a true community space.
