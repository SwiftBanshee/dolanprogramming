Our Story

Rooted in Shawville,
Built on Flavour

Freshly Rebranded

Deano's Chicken has been a cornerstone of the Shawville community. We recently refreshed our look — same great recipes, same dedication, now with a bold new identity. Located on Rue Main, we serve the Pontiac County area with chicken done right every single time.


We are proud to be a local, community-focused spot where quality comes first. Whether you're picking up for the family or calling ahead for Friday's famous order — we are here for you.
