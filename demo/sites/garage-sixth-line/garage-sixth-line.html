About Us

Your Local Garage

Garage Sixth Line is a mobile automotive repair service based in Bristol, Québec. We bring the shop to you — whether you're at home, at work, or stuck on the road.

From light vehicle repairs to advanced Polaris and BRP BUDS diagnostics, our certified technician handles it all with the professionalism and care your vehicle deserves.

No appointment too small. We serve Bristol and the surrounding area and pride ourselves on honest, efficient service at fair prices.
