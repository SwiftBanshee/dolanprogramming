About Us

Located in the heart of Bristol, Quebec, Garage Sixth Line is your premier destination for mobile automotive excellence. We specialize in bringing high-tech diagnostics and precision repairs directly to your driveway.


With years of expertise in both domestic and powersports vehicles, we bridge the gap between traditional mechanical skill and modern digital diagnostics. Whether you are stranded or just need routine maintenance, Jessy Laurier and the team ensure your vehicle performs at its peak.
