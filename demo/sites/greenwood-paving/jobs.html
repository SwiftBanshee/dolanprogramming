Job Opportunities

Join the Greenwood Paving team — Pembroke's most trusted paving contractor.

Join Our Team

Build Your Career With Us

Greenwood Paving is always looking for skilled, hard-working individuals to join our growing team in Pembroke. Whether you're an experienced paving professional or eager to learn a trade, we want to hear from you.

We offer competitive wages, a safe working environment, and the opportunity to work on meaningful infrastructure projects throughout Renfrew County and the Ottawa Valley — close to home.

Seasonal and full-time positions are available throughout the paving season. Apply by email or stop by our office at 177 Mackay Street, Pembroke.

Competitive Wages

We pay competitive rates that reflect your skills, experience, and commitment.

Safe Work Environment

Safety is our top priority on every job site, every single day — no exceptions.

Local Work

Work close to home on projects right here in Pembroke and Renfrew County.

Experienced Team

Learn from decades of experience alongside one of the region's most trusted crews.

Seasonal & Full-Time Roles

Flexible opportunities for both seasonal and year-round employment available.

Open Positions

Current Openings

Full-Time / Seasonal

Paving Equipment Operator

Operate asphalt pavers, rollers, and related heavy equipment on commercial and municipal paving projects across Renfrew County. Experience with hot-mix asphalt preferred. Valid equipment licences an asset.

Full-Time / Seasonal

Labourer — Paving Crew

Work alongside experienced crews on asphalt paving, sealcoating, and base preparation projects throughout the Ottawa Valley. Physical fitness required. On-the-job training provided for the right applicant.

Full-Time

Class AZ / DZ Truck Driver

Transport asphalt, aggregates, and equipment to job sites throughout the Pembroke area. Valid AZ or DZ licence required. Clean driver's abstract preferred. Local routes — home every night.

Seasonal

Line Marking Technician

Operate line-marking equipment to paint parking lots, roads, and athletic surfaces. Detail-oriented and reliable with the ability to work independently outdoors in all weather conditions.

How to Apply

Send your resume and a brief introduction by email, or drop it off in person at our Pembroke office. We review every application and will be in touch if your background is a fit for our team.
