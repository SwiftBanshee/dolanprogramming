About Us

Decades of experience. A commitment to quality. Proudly serving Pembroke and Renfrew County.

Our Story

Pembroke's Paving Contractor Since Day One

Greenwood Paving Pembroke Ltd. has been a cornerstone of the local construction industry for decades. What started as a small, locally owned operation has grown into one of Renfrew County's most respected and experienced paving contractors — without ever losing sight of the values that made us who we are.

We're a family-oriented company rooted in the Pembroke community. Our team takes pride in every project — from a residential driveway to a full municipal road — and we stand behind our work long after the job is done.

From our base at 177 Mackay Street in Pembroke, we serve clients throughout Renfrew County, the Ottawa Valley, and beyond. Our equipment is modern, our crews are experienced, and our commitment to quality is unwavering.

When you hire Greenwood Paving, you're not just hiring a contractor — you're hiring a team that treats your property with the same care and attention we'd give our own.

Quality First

We never cut corners. Every project is completed to the highest standard, using quality materials and proven techniques that stand the test of time and the Canadian climate.

Honest & Transparent

We give you a straight answer and a fair price. No surprises, no hidden costs. Our reputation is built on honest dealings with every client, every time.

Community Proud

We live and work in Pembroke. Supporting local businesses, organizations, and the community we call home matters to everything we do.
