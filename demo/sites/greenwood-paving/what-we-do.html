Asphalt Paving

Our core service. We supply and install hot-mix asphalt for new construction and resurfacing projects of all sizes. From a residential driveway to a full municipal road, our experienced crews and modern equipment deliver a smooth, durable finish every time.

  • New asphalt installation
  • Resurfacing & overlays
  • Milling and reclamation
  • Residential, commercial & municipal
▬▬

Sealcoating

Protect your asphalt investment from the harsh Canadian climate. Our professional sealcoating service fills surface voids, guards against UV damage, water infiltration, and oxidation — extending the life of your pavement by years.

  • Coal tar and asphalt-based sealers
  • Crack filling prior to sealing
  • Residential driveways & commercial lots
  • Spring and fall scheduling
──

Line Marking

Crisp, durable painted lines for parking lots, warehouses, roads, and athletic surfaces. We use professional-grade line-marking equipment and high-visibility paint that stands up to heavy traffic and the elements.

  • Parking stall layout & painting
  • Accessible (handicap) stalls
  • Traffic arrows & road markings
  • Fire lanes & safety markings
▤▤

Grading & Granular Base

A quality pavement begins below the surface. Our crews handle full excavation, grading, and granular base installation to ensure proper drainage and a solid sub-grade before any asphalt is laid.

  • Site excavation & grading
  • Granular A & B base supply and install
  • Drainage solutions
  • Subgrade stabilization
▯▯

Parking Lots

We design and build complete parking lot solutions for commercial, retail, industrial, and institutional clients. Full-service from initial grading through paving and line marking — one contractor for the whole job.

  • New commercial parking lot construction
  • Resurfacing & rehabilitation
  • Curb & island construction
  • Line marking & signage installation

Road Construction & Rehabilitation

Greenwood Paving has extensive experience on municipal and private road projects throughout Renfrew County. From new road construction to full-depth reclamation and resurfacing, we have the equipment and expertise to get the job done right.

  • Municipal road resurfacing
  • Private road construction
  • Full-depth reclamation
  • Shoulder work & ditching

Catch Basins & Drainage

Proper drainage is critical to pavement longevity. We install and repair catch basins, storm drainage infrastructure, and curbs to ensure water moves away from your paved surfaces.

  • Catch basin installation & repair
  • Storm drainage design
  • Curb and gutter work
  • Swales and grade corrections
