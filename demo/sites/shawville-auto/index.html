Us

Local.

Shawville Auto has been a cornerstone of the local community since the 1980s. What started as a small repair shop on Main Street has grown into the Ottawa Valley's trusted destination for mechanical repairs, wheel alignment, and quality used vehicles.

We're proud to be family owned and operated. When you bring your car in, you're dealing directly with the people who do the work — no upsells, no runaround, just straight talk and quality service.

Our team of licensed mechanics brings decades of combined experience. We work on all makes and models, and we stand behind everything we do.
