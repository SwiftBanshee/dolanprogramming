How It Works

Our Process

A straightforward, proven process from first contact to running water on your property.

Step 01

Site Assessment

We visit your property to assess terrain, local geology, and permit requirements before any work begins.

Step 02

Quote & Permitting

You receive a detailed written quote. We file for all required RBQ and municipal permits on your behalf.

Step 03

Drilling Day

Our crew arrives with full equipment. Most residential wells are drilled, cased, and completed in a single day.

Step 04

Pump & System Install

We install your submersible pump, pressure tank, and any treatment systems required, fully tested and connected.

Step 05

Water Testing

Water quality samples are submitted to a certified lab. Results and recommendations are shared with you in a clear report.

Step 06

Final Handoff

We walk you through your system, provide all documentation, and register the well with provincial authorities.
