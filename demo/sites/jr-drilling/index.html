About JR Drilling

Trusted name for quality water well drilling service and exceptional customer care—based in Shawville, Quebec.

We take pride in customer satisfaction and exceptional water well quality. Our clients range from small homesteads to large commercial applications—from hobby farms to high production dairy farms.

We can drill between 6” and 12” diameter wells and are capable of drilling to depths of over 1000’. For homeowners, the average well is 6” diameter and typically ranges from 50 to 500 feet in depth.

We also offer geothermal drilling, pump installation, well rehabilitation, and well abandonment—so you can get the full picture from one team.

FAQs

Quick answers to common questions. If you don’t see yours, send us a note.

How deep is a typical water well?
For many homeowners, wells are commonly 50–500 feet deep depending on geology and required yield.
What well diameters can you drill?
We can drill 6”–12” diameter wells. The most common homeowner well is 6”.
Do you handle pump installation?
Yes—pump installation is one of our offered services, along with guidance on system setup.
Can you drill geothermal boreholes?
We offer geothermal drilling support for loop installations and coordinate around your project needs.
Do you rehabilitate or abandon old wells?
Yes—when appropriate we rehabilitate wells, and we can also handle proper well abandonment to protect groundwater.
