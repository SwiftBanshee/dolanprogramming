Our Family

We Live Here Too

When you walk into K&L Dairy, the person behind the counter is not a stranger. They are your neighbour. They see you at the hockey rink, at church on Sunday, and on Center Street when the snow finally melts in April.

We started this store because we believed Shawville deserved a real butcher — someone who knows their cuts, cares about freshness, and will take the time to help you plan a meal for twelve or a steak dinner for two.

The Pontiac is our home. It has been for generations. We are not going anywhere.
